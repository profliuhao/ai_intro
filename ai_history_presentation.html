🎂 The Official Birth of AI

1956: The Dartmouth Conference

🎉
"We propose that a 2-month, 10-man study of artificial intelligence be carried out... The study is to proceed on the basis of the conjecture that every aspect of learning can be so precisely described that a machine can be made to simulate it."
👥
The Dream Team
John McCarthy, Marvin Minsky, Claude Shannon, and Herbert Simon - the founding fathers of AI gather at Dartmouth College
🏷️
The Name is Born
John McCarthy coins the term "Artificial Intelligence" - a field is officially born!

Fun fact: They thought they could solve AI in just 2 months! 😅
